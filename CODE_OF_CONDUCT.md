# Code Of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, specific, and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and documented requirements.
- Assume good intent, but be direct about technical risks and regressions.
- Share context when proposing changes or disagreeing with an implementation.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or bad-faith arguments
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Disruptive spam or deliberately hostile review behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, discussions, or pull requests that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
